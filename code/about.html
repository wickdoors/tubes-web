About Us

Filmtech Asia is a company focused on the development of technology in the world of digital cinema. We are here to bridge art and innovation, providing solutions that help Asian filmmakers adapt to the new era of technology-based filmmaking.

Through continuous research and development, FilmTech Asia offers technologies such as real-time virtual production, AI-based editing, and cloud-based digital distribution. All of these are designed to support filmmakers in creating more efficiently, creatively, and with global competitiveness.

FilmTech Asia — empowering the future of digital cinema in Asia.
